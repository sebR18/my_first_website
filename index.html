Short Bio

>My name is Jhoan Sebastian Ramirez, I'm a proud, first-generation, college graduate from a loving Colombian, immigrant, family, and I am writing this letter as a genuine reflection of my spiritual enlightenment journey thus far. My life began in the heart of Colombia in a city called Cali. We immigrated to the U.S. before I was two years old so I wish I could tell you more, but my family made sure I would not forget our roots. I was born to two loving and caring parents who have guided me in a way I hope to guide my own children someday. One and a half years after I was born, in the hopes of giving my siblings and me a better opportunity, our parents gave up everything they had and took our lives to the United States. With the help of a couple of aunts living in Bellevue, Washington they had places to stay while they got accustomed to the completely new way of life. I have heard stories, but can only imagen the culture shock they must have felt. As for me, because my life was, pretty much, just beginning, my culture became a mix of both worlds.
